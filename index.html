Introduction

DazTab is a Chrome extension that elevates your browsing experience. Say goodbye to dull new tab pages and welcome a sleek macOS-styled dashboard that keeps you organized and productive.

Why Choose DazTab?

Enhanced Productivity

DazTab helps you stay focused by providing quick access to your favorite websites and productivity tools.

Stylish Design

Our macOS-inspired design adds elegance to your browsing experience, making it enjoyable and efficient.

Customizable

Tailor your new tab with widgets, to-do lists, and more to create a dashboard that suits your needs.

Get Started

Elevate your browsing experience with DazTab. Install it now from the Chrome Web Store.
